Emma Watson's Speech

Emma Watson makes a speech talking about feminism and what it truly means, not the misconceptions of society and how they seem to shun the word now. She shows what it means to stand up for our rights and talks about the need for equality between all genders.

Anti-Fairytale of Stereotypes

This video is an “anti-fairy tale” about two people conquering gender stereotypes and jobs that are for girls or jobs that are for boys. Towards the end it is also explained that these stereotypes still exist today and show that certain genders favor certain jobs because of the stereotypes.


Gender Equality

Unicef shows how women and girls face inequality from forced marriage, to their everyday lives. It talks about gender norms and how boys and girls suffer from them and provides videos and more resources to learn more and take action about this topic.


What can you do?

To make all genders viewed equally we all need to erase the stigmas surrounding each one. A way you could help is by promoting and supporting female-owned businesses. There are still many places in the world that don't respect women and girls or their rights. These are the kinds of places you want to reach out to and give people voices and talk about the women and girls there. You can research and find out which one of our leaders or which organizations to contact if you want to help out in any way with making all genders viewed the same.
