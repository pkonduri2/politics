Why might kids need to learn about politics?

Since 2009 there have been more than 180 school shootings in the US. "We examined 10 years of shootings on K-12 campuses and found two sobering truths: School shootings are increasing, and no type of community is spared." --CNN

In a country called Yemen there are more than two million children without access to education and around 2500 education facilites affected by war, savethechildren.org says.

These are important issues around the world that kids should learn about.
